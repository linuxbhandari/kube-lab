BIGGEST INTERVIEW TIP

This role is architect-level.

They will judge:

Design thinking
Troubleshooting approach
Scalability understanding
Enterprise governance
Decision-making ability

So answer in this structure:

Good Architect Answer Format
Explain concept
Explain design considerations
Explain tradeoffs
Explain best practices
Mention real-world troubleshooting/operations

Example:

“For multi-cluster OpenShift, I would use RHACM for centralized governance, Argo CD for GitOps deployments, and implement hub-spoke architecture with policy-based compliance.”
